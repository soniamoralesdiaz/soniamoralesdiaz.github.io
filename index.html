Urban Renewal in Pereira

Research Social impact of Public Private parnerships in Colombia.

The purpose of this research is analyze a social perspective and the persistence of social issues as a consequence of redevelopment using Partial Plans in Colombia. Ciudad Victoria Partial Plan is the case of study, this plan suggest a social strategy but fell short in the solutions for the displacement. The literature found partial plans exclude user of space, and people represent just consumers for new development.

  • Date: In Progress

Mobility Master Plan (MMP)

Salento, Colombia, 2007.

The purpose of the Mobility plan was to improve the mobility for Salento, Colombia; a small city that has increased the volume of people and vehicles arriving on specific days per year due to the growing tourist activity. The first part of this project is a diagnostic (description of the mobility problems) and the second part is the design project to minimize the problems.The propose was the redirection of some streets the improvement of the more important open spaces and the creation of new nodes that redistribute the tourism around the city.

  • Date: May 2007
  • Category: Academic Project
